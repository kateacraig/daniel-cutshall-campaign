Daniel's Values

Any decision I make as a county commissioner would ultimately center around one question: What is best for the people?

As a county commissioner, I would seek input from constituents, research the relevant topics, and ultimately seek to make data-based decisions that benefit the public. Three guiding principles which would influence my decision-making process are:

  1. Affordability. As our area experiences growth, we need to ensure that people are not getting priced out. Everyone deserves to live a happy and comfortable life.
  2. Environmental Stewardship. We only have one Earth. We need to work together to treat it with respect and care so that future generations can enjoy the natural beauty of our home.
  3. Equity. All people should be treated with respect and dignity. Opportunities should be available to everyone, and special care should be taken to make sure that no group is discriminated against.
